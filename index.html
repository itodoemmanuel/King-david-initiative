About Us

King David is a non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to fostering peace, economic empowerment, and moral values within society. Our mission is to play a significant role in promoting coexistence, enhancing economic empowerment, facilitating the enrollment of youth in skills acquisition centers, reintegrating out-of-school children into educational institutions, equipping vulnerable populations with essential skills, upholding fundamental moral principles, and endeavoring to create a better world for all.

Mandate

King David is an NGO with a multi-dimensional approaches peace, economic empowerment and moral value in the society and to contribute significantly in increasing coexistence, economic empowerment, enrolment of youth into skills acquisition centres, out of school children in school, equipping the vulnerable with adequate skills, upholding core moral values and striving to make the world a better place for all.

Our Mission

King David is a non-governmental organization, operating on a not-for-profit, non-political, and non-religious basis. We are dedicated to alleviating suffering by providing humanitarian assistance, promoting welfare and social development, and facilitating socio-economic empowerment through collaboration with partners.

Our Vision

King David envisions a society characterized by peace and sustainable development for all.
